Work

I design stuff focusing on experience research and cognitive science to inform design decisions which seduces user to interact. Expertise in wireframing, conceptualizing, visually appealing interfaces & apps. Occasionally, I also get my hands dirty with some code to demonstrate prototypes. Btw I am Indian, an apple fan and I do love curries.

Being a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon and pursuing masters in the Applied Learning Science, I am working on projects and research related to online e-learning techniques, analyzing cognitive factors for a visual design and interface evaluation mechanisms. Also my interest span to future sciences via courses like Dexign the Future.

I love what I do and my work speaks for itself. Most of the works and projects I undertake are from word-of-mouth recommendations from my cients.

The work showcased below belong to clients and companies which are secretive and non-disclosed due to IP issues. Feel free to contact me in person for knowing about the processes, wireframes, research and sketches invovled to produce the work.
